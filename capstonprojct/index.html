About Andhra Foods

Our story, our values, our passion for food

Foodies In Andhra #LuvFia

Andhra Foods (FIA Home Foods) was born from a love for authentic Andhra Pradesh cuisine. We are a team of passionate food lovers dedicated to bringing the true flavors of Andhra directly to your table.

Based in Seetarampuram, Vijayawada, we prepare each product fresh to order — ensuring maximum flavor and quality. Our recipes are traditional, our ingredients are pure, and our commitment is unwavering.
